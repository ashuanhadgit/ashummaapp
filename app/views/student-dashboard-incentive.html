   Scholarship Scheme

MyMissionAdmission encourages students to make an informed choice about the college they choose to study in and self finance their education as well. To facilitate this process we allow the students to have video counseling sessions with college of their choice, in the sessions the students are free to ask any query from any college. This platform also gives the students the opportunity to build their scholarship fund, which can be redeemed to pay their college fees.
